About Respect[HER]Tech

Respect[HER]Tech is a brand founded by @LyssaDaCode.

This brand represents the females looking to join the Tech community,
but don't know where to begin or how to include it into their current lifestyles.
Some of us women get called too "ditsy", too "bubbly", too "feminine".
But last time I checked, that has nothing to do
with our dedication to succeed and work in a field that is dominated by men.
Now, I've always been in male-dominated careers.
I was a Car Technician for about 2 years and I always heard "Do you know what you're doing ma'am?"
or "Can I have that MAN assist me?" when in reality, I was more experienced than ALL of my male coworkers.
But, since I'm "female", I get the boot from customers.
I was also an Audio Engineer at a couple of recording studios,
and although it wasn't AS sexist as the Car Technician job,
I still experienced moments where I was excluded from a project because
I am just a girl and I don't have "MALE Engineer Ears"... I though Engineer Ears were Engineer Ears,
I didn't know it was based on gender. I can go on and on, like Badu says. I began working in Tech back in May
and I must say, although I am fully aware of the men who overpower our success,
I still found a passion for it and I don't want to give up on it. I actually want
to combine my love for music with my love for tech. I aspire to take
the "Male Dominated" engineering field and merge it into the "Male Dominated" tech field and TAKE. OVER.
Respect[HER]Tech is here to provide simplified resources and content for your tech journey.
I hope to keep you on your toes and encourage you to join our movement.
